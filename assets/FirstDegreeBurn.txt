FIRST DEGREE BURNS:

-> No need to seek medical attention.

-> Soak the burned area in cool water for 5 minutes or longer.

-> Take pain relievers such as acetominophen or ibuprofen.

-> Soothe skin with aloe vera gel, or cream; apply an anesthetic like licocaine / antibiotic.

-> Protect the burned area using antibiotic ointment and loose gauze
